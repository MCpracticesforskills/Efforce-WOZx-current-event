WOZX Is Here and It's a Big Deal for Energy Efficiency Projects World Wide

read more about WOZX from the Efforce website
Efforce has partnered with Steve Wozniak to launch a new cryptocurrency. It's called WOZX, and it's helping to fund energy efficiency projects worldwide. The coin launched last in December of 2020. It gained momentum on the market quickly, shooting up to as much as 3 dollars per share.
WOZX is a cryptocurrency. It is available on Uniswap, HBTC, and Bitthumb which are decentralized exchanges.
