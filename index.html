Efforce is a Crowdfunding Platform for Energy Efficiency

With the global population growing at an exponential rate, it is vital that both public and private industry do their part to save the environment. That’s where Efforce comes in. Efforce is a company that provides a platform. Their platform lists energy efficiency projects for investors and businesses who are looking to fund energy efficiency projects. These projects increase a company’s efficiency while saving the environment. The amount of energy saved (aka megaWoz just like megawatts except with a cleaver name) is then recorded using smart meters. The more energy a company saves, the more megaWoz they generate. The megaWoz is then distributed back to the company and the investors.

What Does the Energy Efficiency Process Look Like?

Still not comprehending? Don’t worry. Efforce is the first company to attempt using Blockchain technology, cryptocurrency, and energy efficiency in this unique way. Let me give a solid example. Company A needs to re-lamp their warehouse and switch from gas to nuclear power, but Company A doesn’t have enough money to do it on their own. So, Company A lists their project (re-lamping and nuclear power needs) with Efforce. Investors see the project. Investors invest in project. Project is completed. Company A gets new lamps in their warehouse; they function off of nuclear power. The day is saved. Now, all the energy Company A saves is documented on the Blockchain, and each megawatt is converted into WOZX. WOZX is then distributed back to Company A and the investors who funded the project.
